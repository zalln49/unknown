Getting Here

Address
6849 E Osborn Rd, Unit C, Scottsdale AZ 85251 USA

Parking
Two dedicated covered spaces labeled “6849C” are directly in front of the back-patio gate. A third car may use any uncovered spot not reserved for others.

Checking In

Check-in time: After 4 PM (earlier check-in may be arranged—please ask).

A lockbox holds the house key. The access code is provided in your arrival instructions. A pool-access fob hangs to the right of the back-patio door in the kitchen—please return it after use to avoid replacement fees.

Wi-Fi

Network: 6849Cosborn
Password: 5starreview$

Router location: living-room floor, south-west corner behind the couch.

  1. Unplug router, wait 15 seconds, plug back in.
  2. Wait 2–3 minutes, then reconnect.
  3. If issues persist, confirm whether one or all devices are affected and contact us.

Checking Out

  • Check-out time is before 10 AM.
  • Return all keys & pool fob to the lockbox.
  • Place used towels on bathroom floor.
  • Close/lock all doors & windows, switch off lights/appliances.
  • Set A/C to 75 °F (24 °C).

House Information

Trash

Bag all trash and place it in any community dumpster near the unit.

Grill

  • Scrape grate after use and remove food/grease.
  • Turn off the propane at the tank.
  • If needed, ignite with a stick-lighter—stand back safely.

AC & Heat

Summer: keep A/C ≥ 70 °F (21 °C) to avoid freeze-ups. Winter: keep heat ≤ 68 °F (20 °C).

Baby & Kid Items

High-chair • Pack-and-Play • Kids’ utensils & plates Please leave items clean and stored where found.

House Rules

Guests agree to cover damage or extra-cleaning costs (minimum $500). Age 25+ required to book. No smoking, parties, or events. Quiet hours and occupancy limits apply. Exterior surveillance and noise-monitoring devices protect the property. Full rule set provided with booking confirmation.
